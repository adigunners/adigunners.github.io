Mini-League Rules

Game Week Winner

  • No cap on wins: You can win as many GWs as you manage.
  • Score used: Net GW Score = GW Points − Transfer Cost (points lost for transfers are deducted).
  • Ties: If players have the same Net GW Score, the GW prize is split equally among them.

Game Month Winner

  • No cap on wins: You can win multiple months.
  • Score used: The Monthly Score shown in the app — it’s the sum of Net GW Scores for all gameweeks whose start/deadline date falls in that month (GMT).
  • Ties: If players have the same Monthly Score, the month’s prize is split equally among them.
